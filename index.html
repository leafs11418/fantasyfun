Luke's Fantasy Rankings - Super 8

This site will be used to display my player rankings for the 2022 NFL season. Super 8's scoring settings are listed below.

After years of disappointing playoff performances, I decided I need more stability in my scoring. This year, I will be drafting players not just by the overall expected output, but also by the range of their weekly scores.

I decided to use interquartile range (IQR) over standard deviation since I felt the sample size for each player was small and outlier performances could heavily skew the data.

The process:

  1. Using Python (pandas, Beautiful Soup), scrape weekly player stats for 2019-2021 and create dataframe
  2. Apply league's scoring settings to track fantasy scoring
  3. Calculate players' average weekly fantasy score and their IQR
  4. Sort players by average weekly fantasy score (descending) and then by IQR (ascending)
